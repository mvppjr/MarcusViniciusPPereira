Marcus Vinicius nasceu e foi criado no Rio de Janeiro, Brasil. Ele se formou em química em Connecticut College em New London, CT, onde trabalhou no laboratório do Prof. Timo Ovaska, e formou-se com honras em 2020. No grupo do Prof. Timo Ovaska, Marcus conduziu pesquisas na síntese de produtos naturais tetracíclicos, incluindo a frondosina D e o forbol. Marcus vai ingressar no programa de pós-graduação em química na Universidade de Yale no próximo ano.

Interests

Apart from being a chemistry researcher, I truly enjoy teaching and sharing my passion for science with others. At Connecticut College, I developed and piloted ACS Chemistry week, which is an event on college campus to share science with middle school students. In 2015, I also created an event in Rio de Janeiro, called SciFun, that gave 5 science workshops for middle school kids from Paracambi.

This year, I have been tutoring chemistry online at high school and college level. I have tutored undergraduate level General Chemistry and Organic Chemistry for the past 3 years at Connecticut College, and I truly love teaching and sharing my passion for chemistry with students.
